# shelf-life

Digital sanctuary for the "shelf-life" of books.

Honors the quiet time books spend resting on the shelf and celebrates the moment they wake up when you start reading them.

Inspired by personal reflections on books as living companions (see related poetry work).

## Key Ideas
- The Shelf: calm space for resting books
- The Awakening: when a book comes alive in your hands
- Track moments and transformations, not just pages
